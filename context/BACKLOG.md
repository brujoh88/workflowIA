## How to Use

1. **Add**: New items go to "Ideas / Future"
2. **Prioritize**: Move to "Next" when priority is defined
3. **Start**: `/start` moves item to "In Progress"
4. **Complete**: `/finish` moves to `archive/COMPLETED.md`
